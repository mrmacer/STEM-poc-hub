Mission & Vision

Mission

MCIU positions itself as a dynamic educational service agency providing visionary leadership, impactful services, and strategic solutions to maximize access and outcomes for all learners. Its vision: push boundaries to inspire learning, cultivate excellence, and elevate impact across Montgomery County.

Regional Identity

  • Executive Director: Dr. Regina C. Speaker, Ed.D.
  • PA's third largest and second wealthiest county; directly northwest of Philadelphia
  • Funding: ~45% state, ~33% federal, ~22% local/fee-based
  • Nonpublic and PaTTAN budgets are almost entirely grant-funded
  • STEM Council relationship noted; AI addressed in supplemental tech plan
  • Approved: 2025-02-26

Welcoming & Supportive Schools CoP

Responsive, flexible school climate support Community of Practice for member districts. Addresses belonging, trauma-informed care, and culturally relevant education as embedded professional development themes.

Workforce / Transition

Named program led by Joe Walsh with implied transition and workforce relevance. New external career pathway partnerships are an explicit action plan target, though specific employer partners are not yet named.

Data Science

A primary plan goal: applying data-driven resource allocation processes across all programs. A technology director is identified to lead data solution identification and implementation by 2028.

Named Initiatives & Programs
01

Pathways to Empowerment

Named program led by Joe Walsh serving students with transition and career pathway needs. External employer and career pathway partnerships are being actively developed to expand reach and outcomes.

02

Welcoming and Supportive Schools CoP

Community of Practice providing responsive, flexible school climate support to member districts — embedding trauma-informed care, cultural responsiveness, and belonging as professional learning themes.

03

MCIU Learning Network

Internal professional learning structure for organization-wide PD delivery, including monthly "2 Minutes at 2 West" touchpoints, quarterly Lunch and Learn sessions, and Viva Engage weekly communications.

04

Pre-K Counts & Early Intervention

Active early childhood programs with dedicated administrator. Part of MCIU's diversified direct-service portfolio alongside special education, behavioral health, and adjudicated youth services.

05

SE Youth Detention Center Services

Educational services for youth in the Southeast Pennsylvania Youth Detention Center, representing MCIU's commitment to serving students in the most restrictive and challenging educational environments.

Strengths & Challenges

Regional Strengths

  • High-quality professional learning modeling best practices internally and externally
  • Strong employee perception that MCIU supports career growth and advancement
  • Culture of inclusion where all staff can succeed regardless of identity
  • Accessible, efficient problem-solving and strong leadership (stakeholder-reported)
  • Effective coordination of local, state, and federal program services
  • Valued as a specific and customizable LEA service provider

Challenges & Gaps

  • Employee development and PL — lacks comprehensive, equitable PD system across all roles
  • No consistent data infrastructure for guiding budget and staffing decisions
  • Siloed operations limiting resource coordination across programs and teams
  • Communication and transparency flagged as a staff-reported challenge
  • Scheduling and accessibility barriers to professional development participation
  • Alternative revenue development — acknowledged need to diversify funding streams
Plan Priorities
A

Human Capital — Comprehensive PD System

By June 2028, implement a comprehensive professional development system expanding mentoring participation and career advancement for all staff, aligned to MCIU mission, vision, and values.

B

Resources — Data-Driven Allocation

By June 2028, apply data-driven resource allocation processes across all programs, measured by efficiency gains, improved service delivery, and positive stakeholder feedback.
