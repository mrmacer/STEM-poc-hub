Mission & Vision

Mission Snapshot

Mission

IU29 works with school entities, community agencies, businesses, and government partnerships to serve changing educational needs through services, program improvement, and new initiatives.

Vision

IU29 envisions meaningful, purposeful, achievement-oriented learning supported by effective and sustainable partnerships with schools, businesses, industry, and community.

Organizational Direction

Strategic Priorities

01

Stakeholder Engagement

Increase participation of necessary stakeholders across committees and initiatives.

  • Multiple delivery modes for broader participation
  • Quarterly stakeholder meetings
  • Parent and community input structures
  • Stronger communication across stakeholder groups
02

Safety Improvements

Modernize access, fixtures, and security systems to improve health and safety.

  • Building walkthroughs and facilities assessments
  • Threat assessment analysis
  • Facilities modernization planning
  • Cross-department coordination
03

Transition Pathways

Increase student participation in transition and workforce readiness opportunities.

  • Community and industry partner development
  • Work-based learning opportunities
  • Skill readiness curriculum integration
  • Post-secondary readiness and student outcomes

Current Work

Active Initiatives

Active

STEM@Skook29

Regional STEM identity and website hub connecting district support, STEM events, PD, and public-facing resources across the Schuylkill region.

Building

AI Literacy

Supporting districts as they navigate AI policy, classroom use, ethics, productivity, and instructional design. Building district-level capacity and educator confidence.

Active

Student Competitions

Supporting STEM Design Challenge, media and design competitions, engineering challenges, and regional student showcases that connect learning to real audiences.

Growing

Workforce + Transition

Connecting student pathways, industry partners, job readiness, transition programming, and real-world learning experiences across the region.

Emerging

Schuylkill STEM Ecosystem

Building bridges between districts, higher ed, employers, nature and community partners, and regional innovation efforts toward a coherent STEM ecosystem.

Active

Makerspace / Hands-On Learning

Expanding creative, student-centered learning through design challenges, tools, prototyping, and community-connected projects that make learning visible.
