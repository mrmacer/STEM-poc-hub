## Purpose

Creates statewide collaboration for IU math consultants and strengthens the "M" in STEM through shared events, resources, data science, and math identity work.

This workgroup is evolving into a statewide math collaboration space — moving beyond individual IU silos toward a shared infrastructure for math events, professional learning, and resources that every PA IU math consultant can draw on.

## Focus Areas

### Math Events Coordination
Curating and sharing math events across IUs — from local professional learning to statewide conferences and summits. The goal is a shared events layer that every IU math consultant has visibility into.

### Math Identity & Narrative
The "math people" narrative limits students and teachers alike. This workgroup takes math identity seriously — using tools like Story Tables, the *Counted Out* film, and narrative-forward PD to shift how educators and students see themselves in relation to math.

### Data Science Integration
Connecting math work to the growing data science movement — DS4E Summit, computational thinking, real-world data use, and the overlap with CS/AI literacy.

### PAIU Math Collaboration
Building the infrastructure for PA's IU math consultants to collaborate statewide — shared resource lists, joint events, cross-IU professional learning, and a sustained network that persists beyond any one grant or leadership change.
