STEM Signals

Named Initiatives & AI Readiness

01

AI Collaborative Work Group

A regional working group forming in 2025 to formulate and discuss AI policy and practice guidance for all 17 member districts; planned active through August 2028.

  • AI-focused PD explicitly named as an emerging priority
  • Strongest PAIMS signal in WIU's 2025–2028 plan
02

Digital Community Directory

Centralized, quarterly-updated digital directory of community agencies (health, housing, mental health, academic support) shared with all 17 districts. Community liaison model with optional district stipend pilot.

  • Community schools alignment
  • STEPUP PA referenced as existing cross-county initiative
03

Data-Driven Professional Learning

Annual survey-to-PD design loop targeting 3+ new data-driven offerings annually; explicit emphasis on equitable time and geographic distribution across all 17 districts.

  • County-wide and collaborative PD formats
  • Equity of access across all districts as stated priority
Organizational Health

Strengths & Challenges

Strengths

  • Serves 17 districts with broad regional reach across Westmoreland County
  • Positioned as a state-level player and entrepreneurial educational enterprise
  • Existing onboarding framework (HR, supervisor, technology, union sessions) in place
  • Proactive on emerging priorities — AI identified and acted on at plan level
  • Community Schools posture with planned community liaison infrastructure

Challenges

  • High turnover and insufficient qualified applicants — explicitly strategic priority
  • Onboarding framework gaps and lack of engagement infrastructure
  • No centralized community resource directory or dedicated liaison yet
  • Need for needs-assessment-driven PD design; current offerings may not be data-informed
  • Equitable PD access across all 17 districts is a persistent concern

Plan Priorities

2025–2028 SMART Goals

01

Attract & Retain Quality Staff

By December 2028, standardize onboarding and launch 3+ engagement initiatives annually; increase applicants 10% and reduce turnover 5% per year.

02

Community Liaison

By June 2028, hire a dedicated liaison and build a quarterly-updated digital directory of community services shared with all 17 districts.

03

Professional Learning Improvement

By August 2028, deliver 3+ new data-driven PD offerings annually including AI workgroups and county-wide PD events with equitable geographic access.
