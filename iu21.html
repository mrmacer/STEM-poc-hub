Mission & Vision

Mission

CLIU 21 is an educational service agency with a singular, direct mission: helping children learn. Its vision centers on serving as a regional coordinating hub that connects school districts, vocational schools, businesses, higher education, and community groups through a systems-based, innovation-embracing approach.

Regional Identity

  • Executive Director: Dr. Elaine Eib

Strengths & Challenges
